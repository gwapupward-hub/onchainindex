## Brand Overview

**OnChainIndex** is an independent index that ranks on-chain applications by real usage, based on publicly available blockchain data.

Rankings are calculated daily and preserved as historical snapshots.  
OnChainIndex does not accept paid placements, reviews, or self-reported metrics.
