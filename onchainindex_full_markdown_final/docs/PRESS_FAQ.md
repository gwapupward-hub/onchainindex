# OnChainIndex — Press FAQ

### What is OnChainIndex?
OnChainIndex is an independent index that ranks on-chain applications by real usage, based on publicly available blockchain data.

### How are rankings calculated?
Rankings are calculated using metrics such as unique active wallets and transaction activity over defined time windows. Rankings are updated daily and preserved as historical snapshots.

### Does OnChainIndex accept paid placements or reviews?
No. OnChainIndex does not accept paid rankings, reviews, or self-reported metrics.

### Are rankings endorsements or investment advice?
No. Rankings reflect usage only and are not endorsements, audits, or investment advice.

### How is OnChainIndex funded?
OnChainIndex is built independently and supported by the GWAP ecosystem.

### What does “Verified by GwapScore” mean?
“Verified by GwapScore” indicates that a dApp meets baseline credibility signals based on on-chain activity. It does not affect rankings.

### Can developers or partners integrate with OnChainIndex?
Yes. OnChainIndex provides a public, read-only API and embeddable widgets with attribution.

### Where can I find the methodology?
The full methodology is publicly available and versioned at `/methodology`.
